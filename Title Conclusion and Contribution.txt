Title: Conclusion and Contribution

In this project, the database was designed to support the food waste management system effectively.

The database helps in:

Organizing user data
Managing food donations and requests
Tracking distribution of food
Generating reports for analysis

My contribution to the project was designing and creating the database. I worked on defining tables, fields, and relationships between them.

This database ensures:

Efficient data storage
Easy data retrieval
Accurate tracking of food resources

Overall, the system helps reduce food waste and supports better food distribution to people in need.
