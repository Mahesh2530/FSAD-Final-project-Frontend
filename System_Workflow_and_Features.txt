SYSTEM WORKFLOW:

STEP 1: USER REGISTRATION
- Users sign up based on their role (Donor, Recipient, Analyst)
- Admin verifies and approves accounts

STEP 2: FOOD LISTING
- Donor uploads surplus food details:
  - Food type
  - Quantity
  - Expiry time
  - Location

STEP 3: REQUEST PROCESS
- Recipient organizations browse available food
- Submit request for required items

STEP 4: APPROVAL & MATCHING
- Donor reviews requests
- Approves suitable recipient

STEP 5: LOGISTICS
- Pickup or delivery is arranged
- Tracking system updates status

STEP 6: COMPLETION
- Food is delivered successfully
- Both parties confirm completion

STEP 7: DATA COLLECTION
- Data stored for analysis
- Analyst generates reports

KEY FEATURES:
- Geo-location based matching
- Real-time notifications
- Dashboard with statistics
- Impact tracking (meals saved, waste reduced)
- Expiry alerts for food items

FUTURE ENHANCEMENTS:
- AI-based demand prediction
- Route optimization for delivery
- Integration with government food programs
- Mobile app version

BENEFITS:
- Minimizes food wastage
- Supports needy communities
- Improves supply chain efficiency
- Encourages sustainable practices
